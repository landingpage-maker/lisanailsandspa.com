FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Where is Lisa Nails and Spa located? +
We are located at 280 W Kettleman Ln, Lodi, CA 95240, easily accessible with convenient parking.
What are your operating hours? +
We’re open Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 7:30 PM, and Sunday from 10:00 AM to 6:30 PM.
Do I need to book an appointment in advance? +
Yes, we recommend booking in advance to ensure availability, especially during weekends.
Do you accept walk-ins? +
Walk-ins are welcome based on availability, but appointments are preferred.
What services do you offer? +
We offer manicures, pedicures, gel polish, spa treatments, and deluxe packages.
Do you offer nail services for men? +
Absolutely. Our services are designed for everyone — men are welcome and well cared for.
Can I request a specific technician? +
Yes, you may request your preferred technician when booking.
Do you offer gift cards? +
Yes, we offer elegant gift cards — perfect for birthdays, holidays, or special occasions.
Is there a cancellation policy? +
Yes. We kindly ask for at least 24 hours’ notice for cancellations or rescheduling.
How can I contact Lisa Nails and Spa? +
You can call us at 209-333-7888
